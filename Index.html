The Real Difference

Why Not
Epoxy?

Epoxy
  • ✗ Peels, chips, and cracks in as few as 2–3 years
  • ✗ Takes up to a week to cure and 3 days to install
  • ✗ Yellows and fades from UV exposure over time
  • ✗ Prone to hot-tire pickup and salt damage
  • ✗ Absorbs stains, leaving permanent marks
  • ✗ Typically comes with no warranty at all
Midwest Polyurea
  • ✓ 4× stronger than epoxy — built to flex with temperature changes
  • ✓ Installed in one day, ready for use the next morning
  • ✓ UV-stable — color stays vibrant for decades
  • ✓ Resistant to gasoline, oil, chlorine, salt, and rust
  • ✓ Antibacterial & antimicrobial — safe for kids and pets
  • ✓ Lifetime warranty covering chipping, peeling & delamination
